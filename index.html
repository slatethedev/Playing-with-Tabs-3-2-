History of VR

Before VR existed, me and my friends played on a server called CCMC.

CCMC was a pay to win server but it had good people on it and everybody spent their time on it.

I myself bought ranks on ccmc amounting to around $35 in purchases.

CCMC was only semi active like VR is today, but it allowed for people to really know eachother, like in a small town.

CCMC had two main gamemodes, vanilla and modded.

Vanilla mode on ccmc became a good bit inactive, and that's why I lit the fire of vanilla.

That fire can still be seen at my base at 0,0 and symbolises the health of the server.

The owner of the server, Fel was with all due respect, a big jerk.

She spoke to the members in the server with hostility, and for the longest time I wanted to move servers but couldn't because of the friends and things I made on the server.

CCMC had a few world resets to the dismay of the players.

My opportunity came when CCMC announced a reset and decided to also change how vanilla worked drastically

I decided this was a good time to leave, and so I took a world download of everything that was important to me, and left

But what about my friends?

I went to work, and decided that I could make a small, cheap server for me and my friends.

I invited my friends to a discord server I made for my project, and Fel found out.

I was banned from CCMC and any chances of going back were gone so I decided it was a good time to my all of my effort into this fledgling server

It was done, but my friends got bored so I decided to expand and advertise the server, and boom! VR was now a real server!

Now what?

I will be what fel was not. I will be a server owner who is kind and compassionate to their members (but i will /smite you) and never fully reset the world, so players can have their builds for years to come.

If VR ever fails, you will all get the world download.
